Electric Vehicle Data Analysis Project

# Electric Vehicle Data Analysis Project
This project analyzes electric vehicle (EV) datasets to uncover insights on charging patterns, 
battery performance, cost efficiency, and environmental impact. Interactive Tableau dashboards are used to visualize 
trends and support data-driven decision-making for consumers, fleet operators, and policymakers.

# Tableau Dashboards

-  Charging Pattern Analysis  
  https://public.tableau.com/views/EV_projectD1/Dashboard2
    - Provides an overview of EV market distribution and charging infrastructure across India
    - Includes Price vs Range analysis to compare EV models based on cost and driving range
    - Visualizes charging station locations and EV availability across different regions

-  Battery Performance vs Range  
  https://public.tableau.com/views/EV_project_D2/Dashboard3
    - Analyzes relationship between battery efficiency, fast charging, and driving range across EV models
    - Identifies top 20 EV models for taxi fleets based on range and efficiency
    - Shows impact of energy consumption on range, highlighting efficient vs high-consumption vehicles
    - Compares EV brands based on battery performance and reliability

-  EV Model Comparison  
  https://public.tableau.com/views/EV_project_D3/Dashboard4
    - Analyzes environmental impact across EV brands and body types using efficiency scores
    - Highlights variation in energy efficiency across brands and vehicle types
    - Provides insights into long-term cost efficiency (cost per km) across EV models
    - Identifies top-performing EV models for both sustainability and affordability
    - Helps users choose eco-friendly, cost-effective, and high-performance EVs

-  Tableau Story  
  https://public.tableau.com/views/EV_project_Story/EVcarsinIndia
    - Combines multiple dashboards into a data-driven narrative
    - Explains EV trends, challenges, and opportunities in India
    - Provides insights for users, businesses, and policymakers

---

##  Key Insights
- Top "20% of EV models" offer significantly better cost efficiency (low cost per km)  
- EVs with lower energy consumption provide up to "25–30% higher driving efficiency" 
- Significant variation in efficiency observed across "20+ EV brands" 
- Identified "top 20 EV models" suitable for fleet operations based on range, cost, and efficiency  
- High-demand regions show potential need for "expanded charging infrastructure"
  
## Business Impact
 - Developed a recommendation framework for fleet operators that optimizes vehicle selection based on Cost-per-KM,
    potentially reducing operational costs by 15%.
